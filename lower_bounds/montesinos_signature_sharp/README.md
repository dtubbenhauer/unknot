# Higher signature-sharp Montesinos certificates

`run_montesinos_spin_scan.py` computes the unique spin correction term for the seven
higher-lower-endpoint Montesinos targets.  The complete plumbing matrix, spin-class
representative, both orientation values and the orientation used in the paper are
recorded in `montesinos_spin_certificates.csv`.

The five `[2,3]` rows have selected spin correction term `1`, obstructing `u=2` and
proving `u=3`.  The two `[3,4]` rows have selected value `3/2`, obstructing `u=3` and
proving `u=4`.
